Student Visa

For Student Visa Application more shall be advised on the Visiting day.

  • Digital Passport photo and Resume

    With corresponding refrence letters

  • Immigration History

    Copy of all Visa entry and exit stamps on your passport. You need to disclose all the countries you have ever recieved visa rejection.

  • Social Ties

  • Copy of old passport with Immigration details if available

    Birth certificates or IDS/passport for parents and siblings.

  • Economic Ties

    Certified copies of title deeds and assets owned by family

  • Updated study plan (1page) answering the following questions

    Why do you wish to study in Canada in the program for which you have been accepted?
    What is your overall education role?
    Why are you not pursuing a similar program in your country of residence?
    What Research have you done into studies in yout country of residence?
    How long will this program enhance your employment opportunities in country of residence?
    What ties do you have in your country of residence?

  • Proof of Sufficient funds

    Certified Bank statements for the past 4 months for the sponsor.This statement need to have CAD 31,000 or more(CAD 11,000 on top for the first year tution fee)
    Affidavit support for your sponsor
    Source of income from your sponsor
    If employed provide the employedletter and three month payslips
    if running a Business,provide bsuiness registration certificate and CR12
    if there are any large deposits,you need to provide proof of source on income

  • Family Information

    Family Information Form IMM5707

Self-Help Way

This where the Client is hosted by Relatives,Friends or our host. We will connect the client wit the job. The employer will help in applying for the Canada Work Permit at any local government authority by Providing an LMIA Certificate and the Job Offer Letter. This Process takes 1 Month.

N.G.O.s Way

We will connect the client to an NGO and will work as a volunteer. The will be no Salary but you will get some benefits that will help you cater for your personal expenses. You will get and Experience in Canada Certificate. With the certificate and Job offer letter you can apply for Canada Work Permit.This process takes 3 months.
